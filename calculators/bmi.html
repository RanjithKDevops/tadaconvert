What Is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using your height and weight to screen for potential health problems. While not a direct measure of body fat, it correlates with more direct measures of body fat.

Why BMI Matters

BMI helps identify:

  • Potential weight-related health risks
  • Whether you're at a healthy weight for your height
  • When to consider weight loss or gain

Limitations of BMI

BMI may overestimate body fat in:

  • Athletes with significant muscle mass
  • Pregnant women
  • The elderly who may have lost muscle
