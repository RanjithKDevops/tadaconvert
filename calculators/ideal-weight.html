Ideal Weight Calculator

Determine your healthy weight range based on height, gender and body frame size. This calculator combines multiple scientific formulas for accurate results.

1 cm = 0.39 inches | Switch to inches

How to Determine Your Frame Size:

Method 1: Wrist Circumference

  • Men:
    • Small: < 16.5 cm (6.5")
    • Medium: 16.5-18.5 cm (6.5"-7.3")
    • Large: > 18.5 cm (7.3")
  • Women:
    • Small: < 15 cm (5.9")
    • Medium: 15-16 cm (5.9"-6.3")
    • Large: > 16 cm (6.3")

Method 2: Elbow Breadth (more accurate - requires calipers)

Formulas Used:

  • Devine Formula (1974): Most common medical standard
  • Robinson Formula (1983): More accurate for women
  • Miller Formula (1983): Updated for modern populations

The calculator averages these formulas and adjusts for frame size.

Understanding Ideal Weight

Your ideal weight is a range (not a single number) that:

  • Minimizes health risks associated with being under/overweight
  • Takes into account your body composition (muscle vs fat)
  • Varies based on gender and frame size

Limitations of Ideal Weight Calculations

These formulas don't account for:

  • Muscle mass (athletes may healthily exceed the range)
  • Age-related changes in body composition
  • Ethnic differences in body fat distribution
  • Individual metabolic factors

Healthy Weight Management Tips

  • Focus on body composition over weight alone
  • Aim for sustainable changes (0.5-1kg per week)
  • Combine strength training with cardio
  • Prioritize sleep and stress management
